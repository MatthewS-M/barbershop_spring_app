ABOUT US

The Barbershop has been serving the local community for over 20 years. Our experienced and friendly staff are dedicated to providing the highest quality haircuts and grooming services to our clients.

Our Services

Our barbers are skilled in a variety of haircuts and grooming techniques, including:

  • Classic Haircuts
  • Fades and Tapers
  • Beard Trims
  • Hot Towel Shaves
  • Coloring and Highlights

Our Staff

Our staff is made up of experienced and licensed barbers who are passionate about their craft. They are always up-to-date on the latest trends and techniques in men's grooming, and they take pride in providing personalized service to each and every client.

Our Location

The Barbershop is located in the heart of downtown, with convenient parking and easy access to public transportation. Our modern and comfortable shop is designed to make you feel at home, with a relaxing atmosphere and friendly staff.

Owner of the company
Matthew Suprunov

Owner of our company is a highly skilled and experienced licensed barber who has worked his way up from an apprentice to become a successful business owner. He is dedicated, passionate, and focused on delivering exceptional services to his clients, which has helped him build a loyal customer base and a strong reputation in the industry. His achievements include expanding the business, growing the team, and consistently receiving positive feedback from satisfied clients.
